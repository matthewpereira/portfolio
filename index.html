About

I'm a product designer, researcher, and facilitator living in Halifax, Canada.

I have a demonstrated history of user-centric design work on enterprise, B2B and B2C SaaS products, and I love working with multi- disciplinary teams. I've solved problems for companies ranging from small businesses to tech startups, to some of the biggest and most demanding organizations on the planet.

I'm currently a Principal Product Designer at Okta, and Design Lead for the company's SaaS Identity program. I'm responsible for guiding a 26 person team of design and research professionals, charting a course to improve the experience for B2B and Enterprise customers throughout our product landscape. Sometimes this means extending existing functionality, and at other times it means creating entirely new features - but the challenges are always complex, technical, and exciting to solve.

Read more about me

I originally joined Auth0 in 2021 to lead design efforts for their new initiatives team, called Auth0 Lab. This team was charged with figuring out what was next for the internet, and finding the company's next major product opportunities. Building upon that work, when Okta acquired Auth0 I designed the end to end experience for the highly anticipated and award winning authorization products Okta Fine Grained Authorization and OpenFGA.

My company's vision is to solve identity and access management for everyone - from individual developers and hobby engineers to the most complex and large-scale identity use cases for global enterprises. With our extensible and easy-to-integrate platform, Okta's Customer Identity Cloud is already securing billions of logins every year, around the world.

Within my company and the broader tech industry, I'm also passionate about facilitation practice, open source community development, design systems, diversity and inclusion, and accessibility.
